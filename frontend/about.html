üè• Addressing Mental Health Challenges

This project was developed as part of a hackathon focused on mental health awareness and innovation. Mental illness affects millions worldwide, yet access to professional support remains limited due to cost, availability, and stigma. Wellbeing Companion aims to bridge this gap by providing:

  • Immediate Access: No waiting lists or appointments needed
  • Zero Cost: Completely free to use with open-source models
  • Stigma-Free: Private, judgment-free support in your browser
  • Educational: Learn evidence-based coping strategies
  • Supplementary Care: Complements professional therapy, never replaces it

Important: This is a supportive tool, not a replacement for professional mental health care. If you're in crisis, please contact a mental health professional or crisis hotline immediately.
